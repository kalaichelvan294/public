Account Deletion – fin294

Users can delete their fin294 account at any time.

How to delete your account:
1. Open the fin294 Android app
2. Sign in using your Google account
3. Go to Settings
4. Select “Delete Account”
5. Confirm deletion

What happens after deletion:
- Your fin294 account is permanently deleted immediately
- All personal and financial data is erased
- Authentication credentials are removed
- Archived backups on fin294 servers (if any) are deleted
- The deleted account cannot be recovered

fin294 does not retain personal data after account deletion.
